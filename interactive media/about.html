About Our Centre

Learn more about our foundational history, dedicated leadership, and long-term vision for empowering our local community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver comprehensive, high-quality Islamic education rooted in authentic scholarship while fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and supportive environment for all. We strive to empower individuals and families through sacred knowledge, meaningful spiritual growth, and active community service, building a compassionate foundation for current and future generations.

Our History

Established to address the spiritual and educational needs of a rapidly growing local congregation, the Islamic Education Centre has steadily evolved into a vibrant home for worship and learning over the years. What began as a modest gathering space has grown into a thriving community hub dedicated to foundational learning, youth development, and fostering deep, lasting connections across our diverse membership.
