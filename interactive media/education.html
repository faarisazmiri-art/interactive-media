🌟 Ayah of the Day

"Read! In the name of your Lord who created..."

- Surah Al-Alaq [96:1]

Seeking knowledge is an obligation upon every single individual. Let us reflect on the foundational texts below.

📖 Core Quranic Verses (Ayahs)

Surah Taha [20:114]

وَقُل رَّبِّ زِدۡنِي عِلۡمٗا

"And say: 'My Lord! Increase me in knowledge.'"

Surah Al-Baqarah [2:269]

يُؤۡتِي ٱلۡحِكۡمَةَ مَن يَشَآءُۚ وَمَن يُؤۡتَ ٱلۡحِكۡمَةَ فَقَدۡ أُوتِيَ خَيۡرٗا كَثِيرٗاۗ

"He grants wisdom to whom He pleases; and whoever is granted wisdom is certainly given a great good."

Surah Az-Zumar [39:9]

قُلْ هَلْ يَسْتَوِي الَّذِينَ يَعْلَمُونَ وَالَّذِينَ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

"Say: 'Are those who know equal to those who do not know?' Only people of understanding will take heed."

Surah AL-Mujadila [58:11]

يَرْفَعِ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا مِنْكُمْ وَالَّذِينَ أُوتُوا الْعِلْمَ دَرَجَاتٍ

"Allah will raise those who have believed among you and those who were given knowledge, by degrees"

🤝 Prophetic Traditions (Hadiths)

Source: Sahih Al-Bukhari

The Best Actions

"The best among you are those who learn the Quran and teach it to others."

Source: Sunan Ibn Majah

Seeking Knowledge

"Seeking knowledge is an obligation upon every Muslim."

Source: Jami' at-Tirmidhi

Path to Paradise

"Whoever takes a path upon which to seek knowledge, Allag will make the path to Paradise easy for him."

Source: Sahih Muslim

Continuous Charity

"When a man dies, his deeds come to an end except for three: a continuous charity, knowledge by which people benefit, or a righteous child who prays for him."
